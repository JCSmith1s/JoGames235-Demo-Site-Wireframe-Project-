JoGames235, otherwise known as the gaming channel for Jo. He made this channel in January 5th, 2024 as a small project during summer break. Now the channel has managed to pick back up to its gaming routes after being used for music in its lifespan.

After Jo picked backup his channel in early 2025 he is now back to releasing gaming content. This content ranges from blood strike clips, to his "Gameplay To Relax To" series where he plays chill games with no commentary for people to have in the background while they're doing other things.
